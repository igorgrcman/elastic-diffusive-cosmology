\subsection{Part B: Route 1 --- Energy Matching Principle}

\textbf{Definition D23 \Dd{} (Energy Matching):}
At the boundary of a localized structure, surface energy density equals volume energy density times depth:
\[
\sigma = \rho_0 \cdot a
\]

\textbf{Step M1 \Dd:} Membrane has tension $\sigma$ = energy per unit area.

\textbf{Step M2 \Dd:} Surface energy spreads over depth $a$ (core size).

\textbf{Step M3 \Dc:} Volume energy density:
\[
\rho_0 = \frac{\text{Surface energy}}{\text{Depth}} = \frac{\sigma}{a}
\]

\textbf{Step M4 \Mm:} Coefficient is exactly 1 by dimensional analysis.

\subsection{Part C: Route 2 --- Thin-Shell Physics}

\textbf{Definition D24 \Dd:} A thin shell has:

\item Volume energy density $\rho = \sigma/\delta$
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Result:} With $\delta = a$ (core thickness):
\[
\rho_0 = \frac{\sigma}{\delta} = \frac{\sigma}{a}
