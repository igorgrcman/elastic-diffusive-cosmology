\section*{How to Read This Appendix}

Each claim in the ledger has:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Status}: GREEN/YELLOW/RED stoplight
  \item \textbf{Chapter}: Where the claim appears in main text
  \item \textbf{Evidence}: Equation/table/figure reference
  \item \textbf{Tag}: Epistemic classification (Der/Dc/P/I/Cal/BL)
\end{enumerate}

The Decision Log captures \emph{why} we chose specific approaches over alternatives.
The Timeline shows the chronological development of ideas.
